Frequently Asked Questions

Is this medically exact?

No. It is a common estimate, but clinicians may refine the date with ultrasound and other information.

Why does cycle length matter?

Longer or shorter cycles can shift ovulation timing and the estimated due date.

Can I use conception date instead?

This version is based on LMP. A conception-based calculator would be slightly different.

What if I do not know my exact LMP?

Use your best estimate, then confirm with a healthcare professional.

Can pregnancies go past the due date?

Yes. A due date is an estimate, not a guaranteed arrival slot. Babies ignore scheduling software.

Should I rely on this for medical decisions?

No. Use it for planning only and follow clinical guidance.
