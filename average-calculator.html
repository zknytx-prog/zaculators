Average Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates the mean average of a list of numbers. It can help with grades, budgets, performance metrics, and quick planning comparisons.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your numbers separated by commas.
  2. Use decimals if needed.
  3. Click Calculate Average.
  4. Review the average, sum, and count.
  5. Edit the list and recalculate for another estimate.
