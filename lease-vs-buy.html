  4. Estimate resale value if you are comparing long ownership periods.
  5. Use the result as a starting point, then compare total ownership cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this include resale value?

No. This is a simple payment comparison and does not include equity, resale value, or mileage fees.

Why lease at all if buying can build equity?

Some drivers prefer lower payments, newer cars more often, or fewer repair worries.

What about down payments?

You can add them into the monthly math outside this tool or compare total cash outlay separately.

Can excess mileage make leasing more expensive?

Yes. Mileage penalties can change the outcome quickly.

Does buying always win long term?

Not always, but ownership can look stronger the longer you keep a vehicle.

Should I compare insurance too?

Yes. Insurance, taxes, and maintenance can materially change the real cost.
