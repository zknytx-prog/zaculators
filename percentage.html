Frequently asked questions

What is a percentage?

A percentage expresses a part relative to a whole, out of 100.

What is percent change?

Percent change compares a starting value and an ending value to show how much something increased or decreased in relative terms.

What are percentage points?

Percentage points measure the simple arithmetic difference between percentages, which is different from percent change.

How do discounts work?

A discount percentage compares the reduction to the original price, not the sale price.

What about compound percentage?

Compounding applies repeated percentage changes over time, which can produce a different result than one simple percentage change.

Why verify percentage math?

Percentages can be misleading when the starting value is very small or when people confuse points, ratios, and changes.
