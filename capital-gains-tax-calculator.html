What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates capital gains tax from a simplified gain and rate input. It is useful for rough planning only.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter purchase price, sale price, and an estimated tax rate.
  2. Use a rate that reflects your planning assumptions.
  3. Click Estimate Tax to calculate the result.
  4. Review the estimated taxable gain and tax amount.
  5. Adjust inputs if your basis or expected rate changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this calculator estimate?
It estimates capital gains tax from purchase price, sale price, and a tax rate you enter.

Does it include exemptions automatically?
No. Exemptions, holding-period rules, and offsets vary and are not applied automatically here.

Can this be used for property or investments?
It can be used for simple planning scenarios, but actual tax treatment can vary by asset and jurisdiction.

What if I sold at a loss?
The current estimate does not produce tax on a negative gain, but tax handling of losses can vary.

When should I update the estimate?
Update it when your sale price, cost basis, or expected tax rate changes.

This calculator provides tax planning estimates only. Actual tax results, rates, and rules can vary by country, holding period, and individual circumstances.
