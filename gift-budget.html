Gift Budget Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates how much you can spend per recipient based on your total gift budget, number of people on your list, and any extra costs like wrapping or shipping.

Formula / Method Used

Spendable Amount = Total Budget − Wrapping/Shipping Costs. Per-Person Budget = Spendable Amount ÷ Number of Recipients.

Worked Example

A $500 total budget with $40 in wrapping and shipping leaves $460 to split across 8 people, giving roughly $57.50 per person.

How to Interpret the Result

Use this as a starting baseline, then adjust up or down per person based on your relationship with them — an even split rarely reflects real gift-giving priorities.

Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting to include cards, wrapping paper, or shipping in the total.
  • Setting a budget that doesn't account for group gifts you're contributing to.
  • Not adjusting the per-person split for close family versus casual acquaintances.
  • Ignoring your overall monthly budget when setting the total.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this gift budget calculator estimate?

It estimates how much you can spend per person based on your total gift budget and number of recipients.

How do I set a realistic total gift budget?

Base it on what you can afford without going into debt, and consider setting it as a percentage of your monthly income.

Should everyone get the same amount?

Not necessarily. This gives an even split as a starting point — adjust individual amounts based on your relationship with each person.

Should I include wrapping and shipping costs?

Yes, if wrapping paper, cards, or shipping fees are part of your spending, include them in your total budget.

Can I use this for holiday and birthday gifts together?

Yes, combine all planned gift-giving occasions into one total budget for a full-year view.
