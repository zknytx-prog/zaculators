What to do next

  1. Use the number as a starting point.
  2. Adjust upward in heat or hard training.
  3. Watch thirst, urine color, and how you feel.
  4. Do not overdo water at the expense of electrolytes.
  5. Use medical guidance if you have a relevant condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there one perfect water target?

No. Hydration needs vary a lot between people and conditions.

Does coffee count?

Fluid from many beverages counts, though context matters.

Why does activity increase needs?

Sweat and heat loss increase fluid demand.

Can health conditions change hydration needs?

Yes. Some people need individualized advice.

Should I force myself to hit the exact number?

Use it as a guide, not a rigid law.

What about electrolytes?

Those matter too, especially with longer or sweatier sessions.
