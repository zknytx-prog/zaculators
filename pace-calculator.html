Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use kilometers?

This version is built around miles, so convert first if needed.

Why is pace useful?

Pace helps compare runs more intuitively than raw total time.

Does terrain matter?

Yes. Hills and trail conditions change pace a lot.

Should I compare every run?

Only if you enjoy emotional instability. Consistency matters more than one outing.

Can walkers use it too?

Yes. Pace is useful for walking as well.

Why show speed too?

Some people prefer miles per hour instead of pace per mile.
