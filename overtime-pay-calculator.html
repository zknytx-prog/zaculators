Overtime Pay Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates gross regular pay, overtime pay, and combined gross pay for a period. It can help with schedule planning and paycheck comparisons.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the hourly rate.
  2. Enter regular hours worked.
  3. Enter overtime hours.
  4. Adjust the overtime multiplier if needed.
  5. Click Calculate Overtime Pay to view the estimate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this overtime pay calculator estimate?

It estimates gross regular pay, overtime pay, and combined gross pay from the values you enter.

Can I change the overtime multiplier?

Yes. You can use a different multiplier if your policy uses something other than 1.5x.

Does it include taxes?

No. The result is a gross pay estimate before deductions.

Can I enter zero overtime hours?

Yes. The calculator will show the base regular pay estimate.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever your rate, hours, or overtime multiplier changes.

This calculator provides gross pay estimates only. Overtime rules, premiums, and deductions may vary by employer and location.
