Time Duration Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates elapsed time between two date-time entries. It can help with scheduling, project timing, travel windows, and shift planning.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose a start date and time.
  2. Choose an end date and time.
  3. Click Calculate Duration.
  4. Review the estimated duration in days, hours, and minutes.
  5. Update either entry to compare another range.
