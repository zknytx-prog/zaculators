Frequently Asked Questions

What does this annuity calculator assume?

It assumes equal monthly contributions at a steady annual return rate, compounded monthly.

Is this an annuity due or ordinary annuity?

This version uses end-of-month contributions, which is the standard ordinary annuity setup.

Why is the future value much higher over long periods?

Compounding stacks over time, so both contributions and earned growth build on themselves.

Can I use this for retirement savings?

Yes. It is useful for retirement, education savings, sinking funds, and other recurring investment plans.

Does this guarantee investment returns?

No. Real returns vary. This is a projection, not a promise.

What if I contribute weekly instead?

Use a monthly average here for a rough estimate or switch to a tool designed for weekly compounding.
