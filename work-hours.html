Which inputs matter most on this calculator?

The biggest drivers are usually shift start, shift end, unpaid break (minutes). If one of those values is off, the final estimate can move a lot. When you are unsure, run a low, middle, and high scenario so you can see which assumption changes the answer the most.

How accurate are the results?

The math can be very useful, but accuracy still depends on the quality of your inputs and on whether the calculator matches your real-life situation. When should I recalculate?

How should I use this result in a real decision?

Use the result to narrow your options, not to end the conversation. If the answer supports your budget, timeline, or target range, it can help you move to the next step with more confidence.
