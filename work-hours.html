Frequently Asked Questions

Does this calculator handle overnight shifts?

Yes. If the end time is earlier than the start time, it treats the shift as crossing midnight.

How are breaks handled?

The break time is deducted from the raw shift length before the paid hours and estimated pay are calculated.

Can I use this for payroll?

It is a useful estimate, but payroll rules, rounding, overtime, and state law may change the final paid amount.

What if I have no unpaid break?

Enter zero minutes and the full shift length will count as paid time.

Why is the pay estimate different from my paycheck?

Taxes, overtime, bonuses, deductions, and employer rounding policies are not included here.

Can I use decimal hours instead?

This version is built for start and end times because most schedules are entered that way.
