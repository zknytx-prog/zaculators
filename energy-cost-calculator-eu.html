What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a monthly electricity bill in euros using your monthly kWh usage, rate per kWh, and standing charge. It is useful for comparing suppliers, checking tariff changes, and budgeting utility costs.

Formula / Method Used

  1. Enter total monthly electricity usage in kWh.
  2. Enter the electricity price per kWh in euros.
  3. Enter the monthly standing charge in euros.
  4. The calculator uses monthly cost = (kWh x rate) + standing charge.

Worked Example

If usage is 350 kWh, the rate is EUR 0.28 per kWh, and the standing charge is EUR 12, the monthly usage charge is EUR 98 and the total estimate is EUR 110.

Official References

Check your utility tariff documents, supplier notices, or national regulator publications for the most current electricity rates and charge structures. Verify current rates with the official government source where applicable.

Common Mistakes

  1. Using daily or annual kWh instead of monthly usage.
  2. Forgetting to include or exclude VAT consistently.
  3. Entering a standing charge in the wrong billing period.
  4. Comparing the result to a bill that includes other services or fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this EU energy cost calculator include?

It estimates a monthly electricity total by multiplying monthly kWh by your euro rate per kWh and then adding the standing charge you enter.

Should I include VAT in the electricity rate?

Use the rate format that matches the bill figure you want to compare against. If your bill rate already includes VAT, enter the VAT-inclusive number.

What is a standing charge?

A standing charge is a fixed recurring electricity fee that applies whether or not you use much energy in the month.

Why might the estimate differ from my actual bill?

Actual bills may include taxes, levies, market adjustments, regional fees, and time-of-use pricing that this simplified calculator does not model.

Where should I verify my rates?

Check your utility tariff documents or supplier statements. Verify current rates with the official government source where applicable.

Limitations / Disclaimer

This calculator is a simplified estimate and does not model every tax, levy, balancing charge, or time-of-use rule that may appear on an actual EU electricity bill.
