What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates closing costs using a purchase price, a percentage assumption, and prepaid items you enter.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the purchase price and an estimated closing-cost rate.
  2. Add prepaid taxes or insurance if you want them included.
  3. Click Estimate Closing Costs to view the result.
  4. Review the total alongside your cash-to-close plan.
  5. Update the estimate if lender or property details change.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are closing costs?
Closing costs are transaction expenses tied to a property purchase in addition to the sale price itself.

Do prepaid items count toward closing funds?
Yes. Prepaid taxes, insurance, and similar items often affect the cash needed at closing.

Are rates the same everywhere?
No. Closing fees, taxes, and lender charges vary by location, lender, and transaction type.

Does this include every fee automatically?
No. This is a simplified estimate and actual closing disclosures may include additional items.

When should I update the estimate?
Update it when purchase price, expected fees, lender terms, or prepaid items change.

This calculator provides real-estate planning estimates only. Actual closing costs, prepaid items, and lender charges can vary.
