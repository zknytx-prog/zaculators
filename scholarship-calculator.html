What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates how much scholarship funding could reduce your annual tuition and what the scholarship could be worth across multiple renewal years. It is useful for comparing award offers and planning school costs.

Formula / Method Used

  1. Enter annual tuition and fees.
  2. Enter the scholarship amount applied for one year.
  3. Enter how many years you expect the scholarship to renew.
  4. The calculator estimates remaining annual tuition = tuition - scholarship, with a minimum of zero.
  5. It also estimates total scholarship savings = annual scholarship x renewal years.

Worked Example

If annual tuition is $18,000, your scholarship is $7,000 per year, and it renews for 4 years, the estimated total scholarship value is $28,000. The remaining tuition each year would be $11,000.

What the Result Means

The main result is the projected scholarship savings across the renewal period you entered. The supporting line shows how much annual tuition may still remain before housing, books, and other costs are added.

Common Mistakes

  1. Entering a four-year scholarship total instead of a one-year award.
  2. Ignoring GPA or enrollment rules needed for renewal.
  3. Using tuition only when fees are also charged separately.
  4. Assuming tuition will stay flat for all renewal years.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this scholarship calculator estimate?

It estimates the total scholarship value over the renewal period you enter and shows the tuition amount still remaining each year.

Should I enter yearly or total scholarship amounts?

This page works best when you enter the scholarship amount that applies to one year, then use the renewal-years field to project the total value over time.

What if tuition increases every year?

This calculator does not model future tuition increases. If you expect rising tuition, recalculate with updated annual costs or compare several scenarios.

Do scholarship renewal rules matter?

Yes. GPA requirements, credit-load rules, and enrollment status can affect whether a scholarship renews. This page assumes the scholarship renews for the number of years you enter.

Can the remaining tuition result go below zero?

No. The calculator floors the remaining annual tuition at zero for the estimate shown on the page.

Limitations / Disclaimer

This calculator assumes the scholarship renews exactly as entered and does not account for GPA conditions, enrollment changes, tuition inflation, taxable scholarship rules, or school-specific restrictions. Confirm award terms with the scholarship provider and your school.
