Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a bug? ▼

Use the contact form above or email support@zaculators.com with:

  • Which calculator has the bug
  • What you entered
  • What happened vs what should happen
  • Your browser/device if possible
Can I request a new calculator? ▼

Absolutely! We're always looking for new calculator ideas. Use the contact form and select "Calculator Suggestion" as the message type. Tell us what you need and why it would be useful.

Can I use your calculators on my website? ▼

Yes! Our calculators are available for embedding. Email us at hello@zaculators.com for partnership and embedding options.

Do you accept donations or sponsorships? ▼

We're currently supported by AdSense. For sponsorship or partnership inquiries, email hello@zaculators.com.

When will you respond to my message? ▼

We aim to respond within 24 hours during business days. During weekends and holidays, it may take 48-72 hours. Thanks for your patience!

How can I stay updated? ▼

Check back regularly for new calculators and features. We don't have a newsletter yet, but if you'd like one, let us know in the contact form!
