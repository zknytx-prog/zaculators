Percentage Increase Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a new total after increasing a starting amount by a percentage. It can help with pricing, budgets, targets, and quick scenario planning.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the original amount.
  2. Enter the percentage increase.
  3. Click Calculate Increase.
  4. Review the estimated increase amount.
  5. Use updated values if the scenario changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this calculator estimate?

It estimates the new amount after applying a percentage increase to the original value.

Does it show the increase amount too?

Yes. It shows both the increase amount and the estimated new total.

Can I use decimals?

Yes. Decimal values are accepted for both fields.

Can the percentage be zero?

Yes. A zero percent increase keeps the amount unchanged.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate when the original amount or increase rate changes.

This calculator provides increase estimates only. Final prices, taxes, and rounding rules may vary by use case.
