What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a simple flip scenario from the values you enter. It can help with rough planning before you compare purchase, renovation, and resale assumptions in more detail.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the two core project figures you want to compare.
  2. Use current assumptions for cost and value whenever possible.
  3. Click Calculate to generate the estimate.
  4. Review the result with repair, holding, and sale costs in mind.
  5. Update the numbers when bids or market expectations change.

Frequently Asked Questions

What costs should I include in a flip estimate?

Include purchase price, repair budget, holding costs, financing costs, and expected selling costs if they matter to your scenario.

Does this guarantee profit?

No. It provides a planning estimate only and cannot guarantee resale value or profit.

Should financing costs be included?

Yes, if borrowing costs are part of your real project budget they should be reflected in your estimate.

Why do selling costs matter?

Agent fees, closing costs, and other selling expenses can materially change the final outcome.

When should I update the estimate?

Update the estimate when bids, timelines, expected sale price, or market conditions change.

This calculator provides real estate planning estimates only. Actual resale value, repair costs, financing terms, and market conditions can vary widely.
