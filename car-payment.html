Frequently Asked Questions

Does a longer loan always help?

It lowers the monthly payment, but it usually increases total interest paid over time.

Should I include taxes and fees here?

You can add them into the loan amount if they will be financed.

How much does APR matter?

A small APR change can noticeably affect total interest, especially on longer loans.

What is a good auto loan term?

Many buyers aim for the shortest term they can comfortably afford each month.

Does a down payment reduce interest?

Yes. Borrowing less lowers both the payment and total interest cost.

Can I use this for refinancing?

Yes. Enter the remaining balance as the loan amount and use the new APR and term.
