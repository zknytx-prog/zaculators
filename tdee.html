Frequently Asked Questions

What is TDEE?

TDEE is your estimated total daily calorie burn including activity.

Why show a cutting target?

A moderate calorie deficit is a common starting point for fat loss planning.

Is the activity level precise?

No. It is a practical category estimate.

Should athletes rely on this alone?

Not always. Training load and recovery can vary more than the categories suggest.

What about bulking?

You can add a moderate surplus above maintenance.

How often should I update it?

Recalculate after meaningful changes in body weight or activity.
