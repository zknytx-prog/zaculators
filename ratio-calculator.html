Frequently Asked Questions

What does this ratio calculator estimate?

It estimates a simplified ratio between two values.

Can I use whole numbers only?

The simplification works best with whole numbers.

Does it show the ratio in simplest form?

Yes. It uses the greatest common divisor to simplify the ratio.

Can one side be zero?

Yes, but the result may not be useful for every use case.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever either side of the ratio changes.
