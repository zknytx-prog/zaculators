What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a due date using the first day of your last menstrual period and a cycle-length adjustment. It is for informational use only.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose the first day of your last period.
  2. Enter your typical cycle length.
  3. Click Estimate Due Date.
  4. Review the due date and timing estimate.
  5. Use the result as informational guidance only.
