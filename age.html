Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

It uses the actual calendar dates you enter, so the result reflects real years, months, and days rather than a rough division by 365.

What if the birthday is today?

The calculator will show the person’s current completed age and a zero-day remainder for the present day.

Can I calculate age on a past or future date?

Yes. Set the comparison date to any date you want, which is useful for eligibility checks and anniversary planning.

Why do total days and years look different?

Years are shown as calendar years with month and day remainders, while total days is the raw day count between the two dates.

Does leap year affect the result?

Yes. February 29 and leap years are handled automatically by the date calculation.

Can I use this for legal age decisions?

Use it as a planning tool, then confirm age requirements against the official documents and rules that apply to your case.
