Frequently Asked Questions

What does BMI percentile estimate?
It estimates a simplified BMI-for-age percentile range for children and teens based on the values entered.

Is this a replacement for pediatric growth charts?
No. It is an informational estimate and not a replacement for official clinical growth charts.

Why does age matter here?
BMI percentile uses age because expected growth patterns change throughout childhood and adolescence.

Does sex affect the estimate?
Yes. The calculator uses the selected sex because percentile references are not identical.

When should I update the estimate?
Update it when age, height, or weight changes enough to affect the screening estimate.

This calculator provides informational health estimates only and is not medical advice. Use clinical growth references for formal decisions.
