Frequently Asked Questions

Is estimated 1RM exact?

No. It is a formula-based estimate that works better in moderate rep ranges.

Why not just max out?

Because fatigue, technique, and injury risk make direct testing unnecessary for many people.

Can I use high reps for this?

The estimate becomes less reliable as reps climb higher.

Why show percentages?

Training plans often use percentages of estimated max.

Does exercise type matter?

Yes. Different lifts and machines behave differently.

Should beginners chase 1RM often?

Usually not.
