What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a total wedding budget using the categories that often drive the biggest wedding costs. It also shows an average cost per guest so you can compare the event size against the overall budget.

Formula / Method Used

The calculator adds venue, attire, photography, and other costs directly. Catering is calculated as guests x catering cost per guest. Total wedding budget is the sum of all categories. The per-guest figure is calculated as total budget / guest count.

Worked Example

For 100 guests, a $5,000 venue, $65 catering per guest, $1,800 attire, $2,200 photography, and $1,500 other costs, the estimated budget is $17,000. That equals about $170 per guest.

What the Result Means

The total estimate helps set a realistic planning ceiling before you book vendors. The per-guest figure is useful when you need to compare guest-list changes or check whether a smaller event materially reduces the budget.

Common Mistakes

  1. Leaving out taxes, service charges, gratuities, or required rentals.
  2. Ignoring how guest count affects more than just catering.
  3. Using early vendor quotes without checking peak-season pricing.
  4. Forgetting stationery, entertainment, transportation, or honeymoon costs if they belong in your full wedding plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Wedding Budget Calculator estimate?

It estimates total wedding cost from venue, catering, attire, photography, and other costs using the guest count you enter.

Does the calculator show cost per guest?

Yes. It divides the total estimated budget by the number of guests to show an average cost per guest.

Is catering treated as a per-guest cost?

Yes. The calculator multiplies the catering amount by the guest count.

Should I include tax, tips, and service fees?

Yes, if you know them, because the calculator does not add extra venue or vendor fees automatically.

When should I recalculate a wedding budget?

Recalculate whenever guest count, vendor quotes, or category spending plans change.

General Disclaimer

This estimate is for wedding planning only. Actual cost may differ because of contract terms, taxes, gratuities, guest-count changes, overtime, weather backups, and final vendor choices.
