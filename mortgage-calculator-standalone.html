Frequently Asked Questions

PMI protects the lender if you default. Required when down payment is less than 20%. Typically costs 0.5-1% annually.

Interest is calculated monthly on your remaining balance. Early payments mostly cover interest; later payments pay down principal.

Most mortgages allow penalty-free early payoff. Paying extra toward principal reduces total interest and shortens your loan term.

Credit score, down payment, loan term, property location, and current market rates all affect your rate. Shop multiple lenders.
