What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a monthly housing payment by combining principal and interest with property tax, home insurance, HOA fees, and PMI when the down payment is below 20%. It is designed for scenario planning before you speak with a lender.

Formula / Method Used

  1. Loan amount = home price - down payment.
  2. Monthly principal and interest uses the standard amortizing-loan formula based on the entered rate and loan term.
  3. Total monthly payment = principal and interest + PMI (if required) + property tax + home insurance + HOA fees.
  4. The page also estimates total interest over the full term.

Worked Example

Using the page defaults of a $350,000 home price, $70,000 down payment, 30-year term, 6.5% interest, $120 monthly property tax, and $150 monthly insurance, the loan amount is $280,000. The calculator estimates about $1,769.79 for principal and interest and about $2,039.79 total monthly housing cost because PMI is not required at 20% down.

What the Result Means

The large number is your estimated total monthly housing payment. The supporting cards separate that into loan payment, total interest over time, PMI status, and loan amount so you can see what is driving affordability.

Common Mistakes

  1. Comparing only principal and interest while ignoring taxes, insurance, or HOA fees.
  2. Assuming PMI never applies without checking the actual down-payment percentage.
  3. Using a headline interest rate without considering lender fees, escrow, or closing costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this mortgage calculator estimate?

It estimates principal and interest, then adds the monthly housing costs you enter to show a fuller payment picture.

When does PMI get added?

This page adds PMI when the down payment is less than 20% of the home price.

Does the calculator include closing costs?

No. Closing costs are separate from the monthly payment and should be reviewed independently.

Can I use this as a lender approval result?

No. Lenders also review credit, debt, reserves, documentation, and product-specific fees.

Limitations / Disclaimer

This calculator provides a planning estimate only and is not mortgage, legal, or investment advice. Actual loan terms, escrow amounts, PMI rules, fees, and lender affordability tests can differ.
