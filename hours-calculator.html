Hours Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates worked hours based on a start time, end time, and break length. It can help with timesheets, schedule planning, and shift checks.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Choose a start time.
  2. Choose an end time.
  3. Enter unpaid break minutes if needed.
  4. Click Calculate Hours.
  5. Review the estimated total in hours and minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this hours calculator estimate?

It estimates worked hours between a start time and end time after subtracting a break.

Can it handle overnight shifts?

Yes. If the end time is earlier than the start time, it treats the shift as overnight.

Do I have to enter a break?

No. You can enter zero if there is no unpaid break.

Does it show decimal hours?

Yes. It shows both total minutes and decimal hours.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever your shift times or break length changes.

This calculator provides worked-hour estimates only. Employer policies, rounding, and paid break rules may vary.
