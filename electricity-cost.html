Frequently Asked Questions

How does this electricity calculator work?

It converts wattage into kilowatt-hours based on how long the device runs, then multiplies that by your power rate.

Why does the monthly total look higher than expected?

A device that seems small can add up quickly when it runs many hours every day or when multiple units are used.

Does this include taxes or utility fees?

No. It estimates energy usage cost only, so your utility bill may still include service charges and taxes.

Can I use this for appliances and HVAC?

Yes. It works for any device as long as you know the wattage, average run time, and electricity rate.

What if the device cycles on and off?

Use the average run time rather than the full clock time for a more realistic estimate.

Is the result exact?

It is a planning estimate. Real consumption can vary with voltage, load, weather, and appliance efficiency.
