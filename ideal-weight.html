Frequently Asked Questions

Is there one true ideal weight?

No. Healthy body weight depends on many factors beyond a formula.

Why use a range?

A range is more realistic than pretending one exact number defines health.

Can athletes exceed the estimate?

Absolutely. Muscle mass changes the picture.

Should I chase this exact number?

Not necessarily. Context matters more than perfection.
