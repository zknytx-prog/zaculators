Why do units and rounding matter so much here?

Conversions only help when the starting unit is correct. A small mismatch between gallons and liters, inches and centimeters, or watts and kilowatts can create a very different answer. For shopping or rough planning, light rounding is fine. For design, dosing, machining, or exact ordering, keep more decimal places and double-check the source unit.
