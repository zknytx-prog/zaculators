Frequently Asked Questions

Does this use leap years?

It uses an average year length so the estimate is practical and consistent.

Can I compare any two dates?

Yes. The tool works for any valid pair of dates.

Why show months too?

Some comparisons make more sense in months, especially for children.

Will same-day birthdays show zero?

Yes. If both dates match exactly, the difference is zero.

Can I use future dates?

Yes, though that is more planning than age calculation. Humans do enjoy making timelines for everything.

Is the result exact to the day?

The years and months display is a rounded practical estimate.
