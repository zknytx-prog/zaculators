Furniture Cost Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates the total out-the-door cost of furnishing a room or home, including item costs, delivery and assembly fees, and applicable sales tax.

Formula / Method Used

Taxable Subtotal = Item Cost. Tax Amount = Item Cost × (Tax Rate ÷ 100). Total Cost = Item Cost + Delivery/Assembly Fees + Tax Amount.

Worked Example

$3,500 in furniture with a $150 delivery fee and 8% sales tax ($280 tax on the items) totals $3,930.

How to Interpret the Result

This total reflects the full landed cost you'll actually pay. Compare it against your furnishing budget before committing to a purchase, or use it to compare quotes from different retailers.

Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting that delivery fees are usually not taxed the same way as merchandise.
  • Using an outdated local sales tax rate.
  • Leaving out assembly fees charged separately from delivery.
  • Not accounting for financing interest if paying over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this furniture cost calculator estimate?

It estimates the total cost of furnishing a room or home by adding item costs, delivery fees, and applicable sales tax.

Should I include delivery and assembly fees?

Yes, include any delivery, assembly, or setup fees to get an accurate total landed cost.

How do I estimate sales tax?

Use your local sales tax rate, which varies by state and sometimes by city or county.

Can I use this for a whole-home furnishing budget?

Yes, add up item subtotals for each room first, then enter the combined total here for a full picture.

Does this include financing costs?

No, this estimates the cash price. Add interest separately if you're financing the purchase.

This calculator provides cost estimates only. Actual tax rates, delivery charges, and item pricing can vary by retailer and location.
