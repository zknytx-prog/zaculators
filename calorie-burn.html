What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates calories burned during a selected activity using a simplified intensity model and the values you enter.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter weight and activity duration.
  2. Select the activity that best matches your workout.
  3. Click Estimate Burn to view the result.
  4. Review the calorie estimate as a planning figure.
  5. Update the inputs when workout conditions change.

Frequently Asked Questions

What affects calorie-burn estimates?
Body weight, time spent, and activity intensity can all affect the estimate.

Is this result exact?
No. Calorie burn is an estimate and can vary with pace, technique, fitness level, and conditions.

Why are activities listed with preset values?
The calculator uses simplified activity assumptions to generate a quick estimate.

Can I use this for training plans?
It can help with planning, but it should not replace personalized fitness or medical guidance.

When should I recalculate calorie burn?
Recalculate when workout duration, body weight, or activity type changes.

This calculator provides informational health estimates only and is not medical advice. Actual calorie burn varies from person to person.
