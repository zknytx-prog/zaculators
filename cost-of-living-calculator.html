Cost of Living Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates the monthly and annual difference between your current and target living costs. It is intended for simple planning comparisons only.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your current monthly living cost.
  2. Enter the target monthly living cost you want to compare.
  3. Click Compare Cost of Living.
  4. Review the monthly and annual difference.
  5. Update the amounts when your assumptions change.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this cost of living calculator estimate?

It estimates the monthly and annual difference between your current and target living costs.

Does it use official city indexes automatically?

No. This version uses the amounts you enter for a simple comparison estimate.

Can lifestyle changes affect the result?

Yes. Housing, transport, taxes, and spending habits can change actual cost of living.

Is this financial advice?

No. It is a planning estimate only.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever your current costs or target costs change.

This calculator provides cost-of-living estimates only. Actual housing, tax, transport, and lifestyle costs can vary, so use the result as a planning estimate only.
