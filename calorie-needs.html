Frequently Asked Questions

What does this calorie estimate represent?
It estimates daily maintenance calories based on body size, sex, age, and activity level.

Does this account for medical conditions?
No. It is a general estimate and does not replace personalized medical or nutrition guidance.

Why does activity level matter?
Activity level affects how much energy you may use beyond resting calorie needs.

Can I use this for weight-loss planning?
It can support planning, but individual needs vary and safe calorie targets can differ.

When should I recalculate calorie needs?
Recalculate when body weight, activity level, or other major inputs change.

This calculator provides informational health estimates only and is not medical advice. Individual calorie needs can vary.
