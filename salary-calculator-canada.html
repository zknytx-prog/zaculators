What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates Canadian annual and monthly take-home pay from the salary and deduction assumptions you enter. It is intended for planning estimates only.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter annual gross salary in CAD.
  2. Enter estimated federal and provincial tax rates.
  3. Enter an estimated CPP and EI rate.
  4. Click Estimate Canada Take-Home Pay.
  5. Review the gross-to-net estimate shown.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this Canada salary calculator estimate?

It estimates annual and monthly take-home pay using the salary and deduction rates you enter.

Are federal and provincial rules built in automatically?

No. You enter the estimated rates you want to use for your calculation.

Can I include CPP and EI?

Yes. You can enter a combined CPP and EI rate for planning estimates.

Is this official payroll advice?

No. It is an estimate only and Canadian payroll rules and rates can vary.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever salary or deduction assumptions change.

This calculator provides Canada salary estimates only. Federal, provincial, CPP, EI, and payroll rules can vary and the result is not tax or financial advice.
