Salary to Hourly Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates an hourly equivalent from annual salary and schedule assumptions. It can help compare offers and translate salary into a simple hourly figure.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter annual salary.
  2. Enter average hours per week.
  3. Enter work weeks per year.
  4. Click Estimate Hourly Rate.
  5. Review the gross hourly estimate shown.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this calculator estimate?

It estimates an hourly rate from annual salary and schedule assumptions.

Can I change work hours and weeks?

Yes. The estimate depends on the hours per week and weeks per year you enter.

Does it include overtime?

No. It estimates a base hourly equivalent only.

Is the result gross or net?

It is a gross pay estimate before deductions.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever your salary or schedule assumptions change.

This calculator provides hourly pay estimates only. Actual gross and net pay can vary by schedule, benefits, and local rules.
