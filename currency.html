Frequently Asked Questions

Are these exchange rates live?

No. This page uses static reference rates so it loads fast, costs nothing to maintain, and does not break when an API throws a tantrum.

When should I avoid static rates?

Do not use them for trading, final invoices, or time-sensitive settlements. Use your bank or payment processor for live execution.

Why can the result differ from my card statement?

Banks and payment platforms add spreads, service fees, and timing differences, so the final charged amount can change.

Why is JPY shown with fewer decimals?

Japanese yen are usually used without heavy decimal precision in practical consumer pricing.

How often should static rates be updated?

Monthly is a solid baseline for a content site. Update sooner during heavy currency volatility.

Can I use this for trip budgeting?

Yes. It is useful for rough travel budgets, supplier comparisons, and quick planning estimates.
