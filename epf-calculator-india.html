What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a future EPF balance using monthly contributions and an expected annual return. It is a planning estimate only.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter employee and employer monthly contributions.
  2. Enter an expected annual return.
  3. Enter the term in years.
  4. Click Estimate EPF Balance.
  5. Review the estimated future balance.
