What to do next

  1. Use this as a warning tool, not a clearance tool.
  2. Do not drive after drinking.
  3. Plan transportation before drinking.
  4. If impairment is possible, assume it matters.
  5. Err on the side of safety every time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legally reliable?

No. It is only an estimate and should never be used to decide whether driving is safe.

Why is body weight included?

Body size affects dilution in common estimate formulas.

Why is time included?

Alcohol is metabolized over time, so BAC generally decreases as hours pass.

Can food change BAC?

Yes. Food, metabolism, and many other factors can affect the real number.

Does this guarantee I am under the limit?

No. Never rely on an estimate for legal or safety decisions.

What should I do if I drank?

Do not drive. Use a sober ride or wait until completely safe.
