What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates a daily protein target in grams based on your body weight and your selected goal. It is useful for meal planning, training support, and rough nutrition budgeting.

Formula / Method Used

The calculator multiplies your weight in kilograms by a protein multiplier chosen from the goal menu. For example, general health uses 0.8 g/kg, active lifestyle uses 1.2 g/kg, muscle gain uses 1.6 g/kg, and cutting phase uses 2.0 g/kg.

Worked Example

If you weigh 70 kg and choose the muscle-gain setting of 1.6 g/kg, the calculator estimates 112 grams of protein per day. That can then be divided across meals and snacks to support your nutrition plan.

What the Result Means

The result is a daily planning target, not a strict rule that must be hit exactly every day. It can help structure grocery planning, meal prep, and post-workout nutrition, but the right intake still depends on training volume, age, health status, and total calorie intake.

Common Mistakes

  1. Using pounds instead of kilograms in the weight field.
  2. Choosing a high-intake goal that does not match your actual training demand.
  3. Focusing only on protein while ignoring overall calorie intake and diet quality.
  4. Assuming more protein is always better regardless of medical considerations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Protein Intake Calculator estimate?

It estimates daily protein intake in grams using your body weight and a selected intake target per kilogram.

How is the protein target calculated?

The calculator multiplies your weight in kilograms by the grams-per-kilogram target tied to your selected goal.

Why do the suggested amounts change by goal?

Different training and body-composition goals often use different protein targets, so the multiplier changes by goal selection.

Is this a medical nutrition prescription?

No. It is a general estimate and does not replace personal advice from a qualified clinician or dietitian.

When should I recalculate protein intake?

Recalculate when your body weight, training volume, or nutrition goal changes.

General Disclaimer

This calculator is for general nutrition planning only. Protein needs can vary with age, kidney health, pregnancy, total calorie intake, medical conditions, and training load, so personalized guidance may be appropriate.
