Frequently Asked Questions

What does BMR estimate?
BMR estimates how many calories your body may use at complete rest under standard conditions.

Is BMR the same as daily calorie needs?
No. BMR reflects resting needs only, while daily calorie needs usually depend on activity too.

Why do age, sex, height, and weight matter?
Those factors are used in common BMR formulas because they influence resting energy use estimates.

Can this replace professional nutrition advice?
No. It is a planning estimate only and not a substitute for personalized medical or nutrition guidance.

When should I recalculate BMR?
Recalculate when your body weight, body size, or activity planning changes meaningfully.

This calculator provides informational health estimates only and is not medical advice. Individual calorie needs can vary for many reasons.
