Frequently Asked Questions

What is break-even quantity?

It is the number of units you need to sell so total contribution covers all fixed costs.

Why does contribution margin matter?

Because only the amount left after variable cost helps pay fixed costs and eventually create profit.

What happens if variable cost is higher than price?

You cannot break even under that setup because each sale loses money.

Can I use this for services instead of products?

Yes. Treat each billable job, subscription, or booking as a unit if that matches your business model.

Does this include taxes?

No. It focuses on operating economics, not tax treatment.

Why show profit above break-even?

Because owners usually want to know not just survival, but how quickly results improve after crossing the line.
