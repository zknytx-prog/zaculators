  1. Choose the option closest to your real schedule.

  4. Keep your wake-up time consistent when possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a 90-minute sleep cycle exact for everyone?

No. It is a common estimate, not a medical measurement.

Why add 15 minutes?

Many people need a little time to fall asleep, so the calculator includes a practical buffer.

Should I trust this if I have sleep issues?

No. Persistent sleep problems deserve proper medical attention.

Why give three options?

Different people aim for different numbers of cycles depending on schedule and fatigue.

Can naps be planned with this too?

For quick estimates, yes, though shorter nap timing is a different strategy.

Will this fix being tired from doomscrolling?

Sadly, no calculator can override terrible human decisions at midnight.
