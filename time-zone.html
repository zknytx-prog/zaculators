Frequently Asked Questions

Does this handle daylight saving time automatically?

No. This version uses fixed UTC offsets only, which keeps it simple and predictable but not DST-aware.

Why use a static time zone converter?

It is useful for quick planning, hiring calls, shipping windows, and recurring international coordination.

Can the date change after conversion?

Yes. The converted time may land on the previous or next day depending on the offset difference.

Why is India shown as UTC+5:30?

Some time zones use half-hour offsets, and India is one of the most common examples.

Should I use this for legal filing deadlines?

Use it for planning only. Official deadlines should always be checked against the governing jurisdiction’s actual local time.

What is the safest way to schedule a global meeting?

Convert once, then confirm the final local time with everyone involved before sending the invite.
