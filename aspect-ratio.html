Frequently Asked Questions

Why does aspect ratio matter?

It keeps images and video from stretching or squashing when you resize them.

What is a 16:9 ratio used for?

It is the standard wide format for modern video, presentations, monitors, and YouTube content.

Why simplify the ratio?

A simplified ratio like 16:9 is easier to compare than raw dimensions such as 1920:1080.

Can I use this for social media graphics?

Yes. It helps you resize artboards, thumbnails, reels, and ad creatives without distortion.

Why does the calculator ask for target width only?

Once the original ratio is known, one new dimension is enough to calculate the matching other dimension.

What if I want to crop instead of resize?

Cropping changes the visible image area. This calculator preserves the full frame and only scales it proportionally.
