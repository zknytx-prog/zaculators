Hourly to Salary Calculator

What This Calculator Estimates

This calculator estimates annual salary from an hourly rate and schedule. It can help compare offers, budgeting scenarios, and pay planning.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your hourly rate.
  2. Enter average hours worked each week.
  3. Enter the number of work weeks in a year.
  4. Click Estimate Salary.
  5. Review the gross pay estimates shown.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this calculator estimate?

It estimates annual, monthly, and biweekly pay from an hourly rate.

Can I change the work weeks?

Yes. You can adjust hours per week and weeks per year.

Does it include overtime?

No. It estimates base pay only from the standard hours entered.

Does it include taxes automatically?

No. The result is a gross pay estimate before deductions.

When should I recalculate?

Recalculate whenever your rate or schedule assumptions change.

This calculator provides salary estimates only. Actual gross and net pay can vary by schedule, overtime, benefits, and local rules.
